Why Mindliner?

People First

In Mindliner, knowlets and links are individually created, edited, and removed by people, a process that we believe increases information density and reliability.

Clean Knowledge Architecture

We have introduced the term Knowlet for a fact, a belief, an idea, or a decision. We believe Knowlets foster reuse and team thinking better than classic documents. Each knowlet has its own associations, lifetime, and level of privacy.

Communication Through Visualization

From the same data users can choose from a range of complementary display methods: (live) mindmaps are great for structured presentation, "worksphere maps" are great to mapping processes, people, and responsibilities, and lists are ideal for chronologic note taking

Do Not Repeat Yourself

"DRY" has proven to be a vital ingredient of software engineering - why should it not also boost knowledge creation? Reuse lets authors use existing material and shows them what others build with their data. We believe that knowlets are well suited for direct reuse and recombination.

Features & Benefits

Simple Entry

Only the headline field must be populated, the rest is optional. Due to Mindliner's association capabilities knowlets are agile and entry is super easy.

Instant Search

Every addition or modification is instantly indexed and enables fast full-text search. If files with text are dropped into Mindliner (DesktopApp only) a knowlet is created that will be found through searches against any of its content text.

Subscriptions / Watches

To stay up to date users can subscribe to events, people, or knowlets and are subsequently notified accordingly.

High Volume

Mindliner has been designed to handle hundreds of thousands of objectcs per team or active individual.

Quick Setup

Within an hour Mindliner provides your organization with an information infrastructure that operates with affordable cost.

Partners

One of the key applications where Mindliner takes a special interest is knowledge transfer. When key employees move to another project or department the people taking their responsibility are depending on an efficient transition.

Mindliner formed a strategic partnership with Wissenstransfer.CH, a company founded by Benno Ackermann. Benno is a recognized expert in the yet young field of structured knowledge transfer. Togehter we are implementing technology to document knowledge transfer in a way that enables people to easily extend it into their daily routines after the transfer
